Whilst compiling archive for TV productions, you find a lot of things on your travels.<br> These are PD images that I have collected over the years, compliled and ordered to create collections that tell their own stories. When pictures that are not originally intended to be placed together are grouped and titled as though a collection, their intention changes, and we form our own meaning as we search for truth. <br>I'm interested in how images can be used to tell a story, and the way that pictures from the past can both echo scenes from our dreams, help to formulate our desires, and foreshadow an imagined future.<br>
