Public Domain images gathered from over the years.

I occasionally worked in archive for TV productions, and, as I love to hoard, saved images that I liked along the way. I find that, when these pictures are placed together and titled as though a collection, they appear to tell a very particular story, perhaps different from what was initially intended.
